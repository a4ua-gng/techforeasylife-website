The developing pathway

Learn first. Check understanding. Participate responsibly.

For institution programmes, the intended flow places safety education and adult control before practical participation. Exact delivery is agreed with each institution.

01

Identify the experience

A kit-specific or institution-provided entry point connects learners with the appropriate safety content.

02

Complete the learning

Participants review clear, age-appropriate preparation, boundaries and responsibility guidance.

03

Check understanding

A short knowledge check confirms that the important decisions are understood—not simply clicked through.

04

Adult retains control

The school-appointed adult keeps permission and practical control throughout the institution’s approved activity.

For institutions

A shared responsibility.

TEL discusses roles and safeguards directly with each institution before any programme is agreed.

InstitutionApproval and supervision

The institution appoints the responsible adult and confirms its own permissions, policies and environment.

TELClear learning design

TEL provides the agreed educational pathway and communicates the intended boundaries of participation.

EducatorControl and judgement

The appointed adult retains practical control and stops or postpones whenever conditions are not suitable.

StudentLearning and responsibility

Students complete the required preparation, follow instructions and ask whenever something is unclear.
