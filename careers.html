What to send

Show us the signal, not the static.

Send a brief introduction, the area you want to contribute to, your strongest skills, relevant work samples or a portfolio, your weekly availability and why TEL interests you.

Role scope, expectations, duration and any compensation, where applicable, are confirmed before work begins.
